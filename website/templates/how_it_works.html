Our principles

Why Wherelse is different

A lot of comparison sites exist. Here's why we think they mostly suck ... and what we do instead!

No sponsored results

We don't take money from the products we compare. The AI doesn't know which vendor would prefer to win, so it can't be nudged in their favour.

Real people, not PR copy

We read what practitioners and everyday users actually write, not the marketing page that describes every product as "powerful, flexible, and easy to use."

Opinionated by design

Sitting on the fence helps nobody. Our AI is explicitly told to form a view and say who each tool is actually for, not to conclude with "it depends on your needs."

Many sources, one answer

Instead of making you read 40 tabs, Wherelse reads them for you and surfaces the consensus, so you can act on it rather than get lost in it.

Built for decisions

Every comparison ends with a clear "who should pick which" recommendation. That's the question you actually came to answer ... so we answer it.

Gets better with use

The more people use Wherelse, the more comparisons are ready to serve instantly. Your research benefits everyone who asks the same question after you.

FAQ

Common questions

How up to date are the comparisons?
Brand new comparisons are researched fresh the moment you ask. For comparisons that have been run before, you're getting a recent snapshot. We periodically refresh popular ones to stay current as products evolve.
Can I compare anything, or just software?
Wherelse works best for software tools, SaaS products, developer platforms, and productivity apps. Places where there's a rich online conversation to draw from. Products with little community discussion online will produce thinner results.
How long does a new comparison take?
Usually 10-30 seconds for something we haven't seen before. We're gathering and reading a lot in that time. If the pair has been compared before, it loads near-instantly.
Can I trust the verdict?
The verdict reflects what people have written online about these tools and we're transparent about that. We don't take money from vendors, and the AI is instructed to be honest rather than diplomatic. Treat it as a well-informed starting point, not gospel.
Do I need an account?
No. Comparisons are free and open to everyone. An account lets you save comparisons to your history and revisit them later but signing up is never required to use the core feature.
